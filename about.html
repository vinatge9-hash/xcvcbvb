Our Story

The Daily Grind began with a simple idea: to create a gathering place where coffee, craft, and community come together. From our first brew to today, we’ve pursued quality, hospitality, and a warm, welcoming environment for all who walk through our door.

Meet the Team

We’re a small, passionate crew who believe great coffee starts with great people. Our founders built The Daily Grind to be a neighborhood hub where conversations brew as richly as the coffee.

From sourcing beans with care to crafting each drink by hand, we’re committed to sustainability, warmth, and a consistent, high-quality experience for every guest who sits at our bar.
